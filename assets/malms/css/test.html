WHAT IS CENTRIN ONLINE ?

PT Centrin Online Prima is currently one of Indonesia Internet Service Provider (ISP) pioneer, and one of the largest ISP in the country. The company changed its name from PT Khasanah Timur Indonesia (KTI) to PT Centrin Online Prima (COP) according to the notarial deed made by Kirana Ivyminerva Wilamarta,S.H.,LL.M Nomor 1 dated November 18, 2013, Surat Keputusan Menteri Hukum dan Hak Asasi Manusia Republik Indonesia Nomor AHU-11696.AH.01.02 tahun 2014. COP founded in 1996 and has Internet Service Provider license from Indonesia government No. KM.97/PT/102/MPPT-96, also have the ITKP certifikation from Direktur Jenderal Pos dan Telekomunikasi No. 330/Dirjen/2005.
